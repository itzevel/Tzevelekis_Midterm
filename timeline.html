The Evolution of Porsche: A Timeline

  • 1948 - Porsche 356

    The Porsche 356 was the first production automobile by Porsche. It remained in production for over 15 years.

  • 1964 - Porsche 911

    Arguably the most iconic Porsche, the 911 was introduced in 1964 and continues to be a major part of Porsche’s lineup.

  • 2020 - Porsche Taycan

    The Taycan represents Porsche’s entry into electric vehicles, continuing their innovation in performance and luxury.
